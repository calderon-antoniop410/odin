World's Best Lasagna


Recipe Description: Really customizable lasgana recipe to your liking.

Ingredients List

  • Meat - Ground Beef or Sausage works best with this recipe.
  • Onions/Garlic - 1 Onion and 2 cloves of garlic.
  • Tomato Products - 1 Can crushed tomatos, 2 cans of tomato sauce, 2 cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar - 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • Spices/Seasonings - Parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, black pepper.
  • Lasagna Noodles - Any noodles are fine.
  • Cheeses - Any cheese of preference, but recommend: parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese.
  • Egg - Just one egg

How to make the dish

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Cook Noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna like so
    1. Meat Sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta Mixture
    4. Mozarella Slices
    5. Meat Sauce
    6. Parmesan Cheese
    7. Repeat layers, than finish top with remaining Parmesan
  5. Cover with foil and bake- The assembled lasagna should take about 50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F.
    Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes. Also, it's important to let the lasagna
    rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before you cut into it.
